Midweek Practices

  • Midweek shoots are held approximately monthly - early in the month from September to December and later in the month the rest of the year (to try and avoid the really cold weather in winter, often without success!)
  • Dates for midweek shooting will be published in this BRC Website Calendar at least 3 months in advance but they are subject to change depending on people’s availability. We usually shoot on a Tuesday, when NRA target hire fees are discounted. All confirmed midweek dates will be advertised across club membership approximately 2-3 weeks in advance via WhatsApp.
  • These shoots are open to all Club members, Bedford School students and Old Bedfordians and the format for each day is decided amongst those attending. They are a perfect opportunity to practice or be coached for all shooting abilities.

School and BRC Practices

  • These dates will be scheduled by the school (1 per term)
  • Shooting will be in the afternoon for both BRC and the school, with potential for training / workshops in the morning
  • BRC to try and provide coaching for the school

Fram Matches

  • The Framlingham Short Range, All Day and Long Range matches will be used as club shooting dates / targets, accommodating both coached and individual shooting – you choose.

Individual Events

  • These are for information only but we will endeavour to improve communication and hopefully encourage more club member participation in these events. If further dates come to our attention we will add them to the calendar.

Further Information

  • For more details on Bedfordians events, contact us at contact@bedfordiansrc.org
  • Details of many other target rifle events and their entry forms can be found at https://shootr.app/
