Midweek Practices

  • In order to try and get better weather the 2024 Jan to June dates are the last Wed in each month, the September to December dates are the first Wednesday in each month
  • All MP dates are provisional and could be rescheduled / cancelled depending on the weather forecast and availability of those interested in shooting
  • Shooting format for each day to be decided between those attending

School and BRC Practices

  • These dates will be scheduled by the school (1 per term)
  • Shooting will be in the afternoon for both BRC and the school, with potential for training / workshops in the morning
  • BRC to try and provide coaching for the school

Fram Matches

  • The Framlingham Short Range, All Day and Long Range matches will be used as club shooting dates / targets, accommodating both coached and individual shooting – you choose.

Individual Events

  • These are for information only but we will endeavour to improve communication and hopefully encourage more club member participation in these events. If further dates come to our attention we will add them to the calendar.
